Choose Your Own Adventure By: Reed Goodwin, Andrew Nilsson, Sam Laguna, Brandon Le.

Ending


You continue to wander around in the darkness, unsure of the amount of time that has passed or how far you have traveled. You keep walking, hoping that you will come across something familiar, like a wall, or a door. You keep wandering, unable to stop. Becoming more and more panicked and more and more hopeless. You wander forever.
